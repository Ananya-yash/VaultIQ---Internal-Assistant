  WHAT IS VAULTIQ?
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  VaultIQ is a secure, role-based AI assistant that answers
  questions using your company's internal documents.

  ACCESS RULES
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━
  • Each user can only query documents from their own department
    plus shared documents.
  • Admin has access to all departments.
  • Tokens expire after 8 hours — log in again if your session ends.

  SAMPLE QUESTIONS TO ASK
━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━━

  ┌─ ENGINEERING ──────────────────────────────────────────────────┐

  1. How is the data layer segmented among PostgreSQL, MongoDB, and
     Redis to handle different types of workloads?

  2. What are the primary frameworks and languages used for the
     frontend, mobile, and backend layers?

  3. What automated checks must be met before a pull request is
     eligible for code review?

  4. How does the system implement data protection and encryption
     for data in transit and at rest?

  5. What is the resolution SLA for a defect classified as
     S1 (Critical)?

  6. What deployment strategy is used when releasing code to
     Production?

  7. What are the three primary features targeted for short-term
     AI and Machine Learning integration?

  └────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  ┌─ FINANCE ──────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐

  1. Revenue grew 28% while net income rose only 12% — which
     vendor expense drove this discrepancy?

  2. Annual gross margin rose from 55% to 64%; what operational
     strategy was credited for this 9-point improvement?

  3. With a DSO of 45 days against a 30-day benchmark, what
     mitigation plan was implemented in Q3 to accelerate
     receivables?

  4. Q2 focused on Southeast Asian influencer marketing while Q3
     targeted Latin American loyalty programs — which region faced
     higher vendor cost inflation?

  5. Annual investing activities reached $260 million; how did the
     Q4 investment peak correlate with that quarter's financing
     cash inflows?

  └────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  ┌─ HR ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐

  1. Which employee in the Technology department has a Performance
     Rating of 1 but earns above 1,500,000? What is their role
     and location?

  2. Which employee has the highest Attendance Percentage (99.98%)?
     List their name, department, and Performance Rating.

  3. In the Finance department, which employee has taken the most
     leaves (26), and what is their remaining Leave Balance?

  4. Which location has the highest concentration of Relationship
     Managers, and what is the average Performance Rating for that
     role in that city?

  5. Employee FINEMP1007 manages several people — who among their
     reports has the earliest joining date, and what is that
     employee's current salary?

  └────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  ┌─ MARKETING ────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐

  1. Between Q1 and Q3, FinNova targeted Europe, Southeast Asia,
     and Latin America. In which country did "regulatory delays"
     hinder acquisition targets, and how did the next quarter's
     strategy aim to resolve this?

  2. Q2 2024 saw the highest marketing spend at $2.5 million. How
     did the targeted CPA in Q2 compare to Q3, and what does this
     shift indicate about marketing efficiency?

  3. Which initiative — InstantPay (Q1) or the loyalty program
     (Q3) — resulted in higher initial sign-ups, and what was the
     percentage over-performance?

  4. In which quarter did actual Conversion Rate performance fail
     to meet its target, and what optimization was recommended?

  5. Q3 targets a 3.75x ROI. Comparing Q1 and Q3 preliminary ROI,
     which quarter was closer to its target, and what was Q3's
     remaining revenue gap?

  └────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘

  ┌─ SHARED ───────────────────────────────────────────────────────┐

  1. Which benefit requires exactly 5 years of continuous service,
     and what is the legal minimum percentage for an annual bonus?

  2. After how many consecutive sick days is a medical certificate
     mandatory, and how many late arrivals per month trigger
     disciplinary action?

  3. Within how many working days must a salary discrepancy be
     reported to HR, and what is the standard resolution timeframe?

  4. What is the maximum annual reimbursement for industry
     certifications, and how many years must an employee serve
     post-payment to avoid repayment?

  5. What is the maximum duration for processing a Full & Final
     Settlement, and which 2023 Act governs employee data privacy?

  └────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────┘
